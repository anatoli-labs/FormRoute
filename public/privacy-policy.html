Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 25, 2025
Last Updated: August 25, 2025

1. Introduction

FormRoute ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Anton Savytski of Anatoli Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our FormRoute service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use FormRoute's dashboard to create forms, we collect:

  • Form Configuration Data: Form names, success messages, redirect URLs, email templates
  • SMTP Credentials: Email server settings, usernames, passwords, sender/recipient email addresses
  • Database Configuration: Database connection settings and API keys for third-party services

2.2 Form Submission Data (Pass-Through Only)

Privacy-First Architecture: FormRoute does not store form submission data. When end users submit forms, we process and route the data to your configured services without permanent storage:

  • Form Data: Routed directly to your chosen database or webhook service
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, user agents, and timestamps are included in the data sent to your services
  • Email Processing: Form data is used to send email notifications then discarded

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage analytics and service performance metrics
  • Error logs and debugging information
  • Browser and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Operations

  • Form Processing: To process and route form submissions according to your configuration
  • Email Delivery: To send email notifications using your SMTP credentials
  • Data Storage: To store form submissions in your specified databases

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing service usage to improve performance and features
  • Debugging and resolving technical issues
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

FormRoute integrates with third-party services you configure:

  • Email Services: Your SMTP credentials are used to send emails through your chosen provider
  • Database Services: Form data is stored in databases you configure (Google Sheets, Turso, etc.)
  • Webhook Services: Form data is sent to webhook URLs you specify (Make.com, Zapier, etc.)
  • Custom Webhooks: When you use custom webhooks, your credentials are encoded in your form URLs and never stored in our database

4.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures:

  • Encryption: SMTP credentials and sensitive data are encrypted in our database
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure cloud platforms with industry-standard protections

Important: While we implement security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You provide SMTP credentials at your own risk.

6. Data Retention

  • Form Configurations: Retained until you delete your forms
  • Form Submissions: Not stored by FormRoute - data is immediately routed to your configured services
  • SMTP Credentials: Stored securely and deleted when you remove email configuration
  • Custom Webhook Credentials: Never stored in our database - encoded in your form URLs only
  • Logs: Technical logs are retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your forms and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request export of your form configurations

8. Cookies and Tracking

FormRoute uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential cookies for service functionality
  • No third-party advertising cookies
  • No behavioral tracking for marketing purposes

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

FormRoute is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
